# Legend

- [+160]: Red - Instant Perforation of Eardrum
-  [159-120 db]: Orange - Immediate Hearing Damage possible
-  [120 - 85 db]: Yellow - Hearing damage within minutes to hours
- [85 - 0 db]: While - Safe
